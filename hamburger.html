How 2 Make a Hamborger

Ingredients

  • Hamborger meat (horse preferred)
  • Buns
  • Ketchup or Catsup (a very confusing difference)
  • pickles (like the cat)

Steps:

  1. Step 1. Unpackage all your foodstuffs.
  2. Step 2. Put hamborger meat on the grill.
  3. Step 3. Turn on grill (highest setting so it goes faster).
  4. Step 4. While hamborgers is cooking, chop up pickes (might get loud).
  5. Step 5. Once hamborges is done cooking (will be black or brown after a few mins), put on bun.
  6. Step 6. Assemble all ingredients, put ketchup (catsup?) on top of bun.
